How Stira Works

You Tell Stira What You Want

Open the command bar with a quick shortcut (Cmd+T), then speak or type your intent. Stira parses your natural language into context-aware actions.

⌘ T

Stira Reads Your Screen

Using secure AI screen analysis and DOM parsing, Stira understands what's on the page — video titles, sidebar elements, comments, articles, ads, and more.

It Filters or Highlights

Want to focus? Stira hides distractions. Want to learn? Stira highlights relevant content. Want to explore a niche? Stira surfaces the most aligned results.

You Stay In Flow

Stira works invisibly in the background. No switching tabs. No configuration. Just results that match what you asked for.

Stira Improves Over Time

Stira learns from your interactions and becomes smarter with every use. The more you use it, the better it gets at understanding your needs.
