Meet Stira — AI That Understands How You Want to Use the Web
Stira is a browser extension powered by real-time AI that helps you stay focused, learn faster, and avoid distractions — without needing to switch apps, toggle modes, or set up complicated rules.

Just tell Stira what you want to do:

“I want to study linear algebra.”
“Only show NBA highlights on YouTube.”
“Block social media for 30 minutes.”

Stira instantly adapts the web to your goal.
No overengineering. No rigid presets. Just smart, fluid support — when and where you need it.
